About Sip & Crunch

Our Story

Sip & Crunch was born from a simple idea: what if we could enjoy our beverages and eat the cup too? Founded with a mission to reduce single-use waste, we've developed innovative edible cups that are both delicious and durable.

Our team of food scientists and environmental enthusiasts worked tirelessly to create cups that hold hot and cold beverages while maintaining their structure and adding a delightful taste to your drink.

Today, we're proud to offer sustainable alternatives to traditional cups for both individual consumers and businesses committed to reducing their environmental footprint.

Our Mission

At Sip & Crunch, we're committed to creating innovative, sustainable products that make everyday actions more environmentally friendly without sacrificing convenience or enjoyment.

We believe that small changes can make a big difference. By choosing an edible cup instead of a disposable one, you're helping reduce waste and showing that sustainability can be delicious!
